STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: WEATHER SURGE (EXTREME WEATHER OPERATIONS)
ID: SOP-OPS-006
Status: Approved

Operational Context:
This SOP is activated during heavy rainfall, flooding, extreme heatwaves, or other weather disturbances that impact delivery courier speeds and courier availability.

Corrective Actions:
1. Restrict delivery operations to a maximum 2.5km radius from the store to ensure rider safety and reduce exposure.
2. Enable "Weather Surcharge" (+30%) payout bonuses to encourage available delivery partners to log in.
3. Automatically swap to double-bag thermal packaging to protect food from rain and heat.
4. Notify customers on digital portals of extended delivery times (+20 mins) due to adverse weather safety protocols.

Expected Recovery Improvement: 30% reduction in delay rate and safety index normalization.
SOP Author: Safety & Operations Director
