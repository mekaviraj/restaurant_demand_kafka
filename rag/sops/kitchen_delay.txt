STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: KITCHEN DELAY (KITCHEN BOTTLENECK)
ID: SOP-OPS-001
Status: Approved

Operational Context:
This SOP is activated when the Average Preparation Time exceeds 25 minutes or a kitchen bottleneck is identified.

Corrective Actions:
1. Add one cook to Station B (Prep/Assembly Line) to assist with order bottlenecking.
2. Prioritize high-volume / high-frequency dishes (e.g., standard burgers and simple biryani) to clear the queue.
3. Temporarily pause low-demand/high-prep time items (e.g., gourmet custom items or platters) from the digital menu.
4. Notify delivery couriers to wait an additional 10 minutes before arriving at the restaurant to minimize idle time.

Expected Recovery Improvement: 28% reduction in preparation delay.
SOP Author: Operations Director
