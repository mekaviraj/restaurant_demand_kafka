STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: COURIER DELAY (DELIVERY PARTNER SHORTAGE)
ID: SOP-OPS-002
Status: Approved

Operational Context:
This SOP is activated when the Delay Rate is high but preparation times are normal (under 18 mins), indicating order backlog is due to a shortage of delivery partners (couriers).

Corrective Actions:
1. Increase the delivery payout multiplier to 1.5x for active riders to attract nearby partners.
2. Route high-priority orders to third-party premium logistics networks (e.g., Shadowfax, Borzoi) to clear pending orders.
3. Automatically stack up to 2 orders per delivery courier if they are heading to the same delivery neighborhood.
4. Notify customers of an updated delivery timeline (+15 mins) and issue a 10% discount coupon for the delay.

Expected Recovery Improvement: 22% reduction in delivery delay.
SOP Author: Logistics Lead
