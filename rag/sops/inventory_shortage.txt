STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: INVENTORY SHORTAGE (RAW INGREDIENT DEFICIT)
ID: SOP-OPS-005
Status: Approved

Operational Context:
This SOP is activated when the Cancellation Rate is high (above 20%) due to out-of-stock items, or when key kitchen ingredients are depleted.

Corrective Actions:
1. Conduct an immediate automated count of stock in the walk-in cooler and pantry.
2. Mark depleted items as "Unavailable" on all digital delivery platforms to prevent future customer cancellations.
3. Automatically offer pre-approved substitutes to customers (e.g., offer paneer dishes if chicken is out, or alternative beverages).
4. Direct nearest local vendor supply orders via express courier services for emergency replenishment of core ingredients.

Expected Recovery Improvement: 35% reduction in customer cancellation rates.
SOP Author: Inventory Control Lead
